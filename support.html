Common Issues & Solutions

App Not Loading

  • Refresh the Zoom Apps panel
  • Restart your Zoom client
  • Check that you're in an active meeting

Overlay Not Showing

  • Click the overlay mode button to cycle through OFF/MINI/FULL
  • Ensure you have a current speaker active
  • Check that your Zoom client supports video overlay features

Participants Not Appearing

  • Wait a few seconds for participant list to load
  • Ensure participants have joined the meeting (not waiting room)
  • Try clicking the refresh button if available

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space or E - End current turn
  • N - Next speaker
  • P - Pause/Resume timer
  • G - Add 15 seconds grace period

FAQ

Does Queue Time store my data?

No. Queue Time operates entirely within your Zoom client and stores no personal or meeting data.

Can I use Queue Time in webinars?

Queue Time is designed for meetings where participants can unmute. Webinar support may be limited.

Is Queue Time free?

Yes, Queue Time is completely free to use.
