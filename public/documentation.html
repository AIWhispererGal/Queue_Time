Queue Time Documentation

Complete guide for installing, using, and removing Queue Time in Zoom meetings.

Installation

Step 1: Add Queue Time to Zoom

  1. Click the installation link provided or search "Queue Time" in Zoom App Marketplace
  2. Click "Add" or "Install"
  3. Review permissions and click "Authorize"
  4. Queue Time is now available in your Zoom Apps panel

Step 2: Access During Meetings

  1. Join or start a Zoom meeting
  2. Click the "Apps" button in the toolbar
  3. Select "Queue Time" from your apps
  4. The app will open in the side panel

Using Queue Time

Basic Operation

  1. Add Speakers: Click participant names to add them to the queue
  2. Reorder: Drag and drop speakers to change order
  3. Start: Click "Start" or press N to begin with first speaker
  4. Next Speaker: Press Space to end turn and advance

Timer Controls

  • Set Time Limit: Use the Settings panel (30s, 1m, 2m, 5m, or custom)
  • Pause/Resume: Press P or click pause button
  • Grace Period: Press G to add 15 seconds
  • End Turn: Press Space or E

Video Overlay Modes

Click the overlay button to cycle through:

  • OFF: No overlay on video
  • MINI: Large timer only
  • FULL: Timer, queue, stats, and keyboard hints
Note: Video overlay requires a supported Zoom client version and may not be available in all meeting types.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Key Action
Space / E End current turn
N Next speaker (start if none active)
P Pause/Resume timer
G Add 15 seconds grace period

Statistics Panel

Click "Statistics" header to expand/collapse. Shows:

  • Total session time
  • Number of turns taken
  • Individual speaker statistics
  • Average time per speaker

Advanced Features

End Turn vs End Topic

  • End Turn: Moves to next speaker, keeps queue and stats
  • End Topic: Clears queue for new discussion topic

Queue Management

  • Click the X next to any name to remove from queue
  • Drag names to reorder
  • Current speaker appears at top with green highlight
  • Queue shows remaining speakers in order

Removing Queue Time

Temporary Removal (Close App)

  1. Click the X in the app panel during meeting
  2. App closes but remains installed

Complete Uninstall

  1. Go to zoom.us/profile/apps
  2. Find "Queue Time" in your installed apps
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. Confirm removal

Troubleshooting

App Not Loading

  • Ensure you're in an active meeting
  • Try refreshing the Apps panel
  • Restart Zoom client if needed

Overlay Not Visible

  • Check overlay mode (should be MINI or FULL)
  • Ensure a speaker is active
  • Some Zoom versions may not support overlay

Privacy & Security

Queue Time:

  • Does NOT store any meeting or user data
  • Does NOT record or transmit information
  • Operates entirely within your Zoom client
  • No account or sign-up required
