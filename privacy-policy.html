Privacy Policy for Queue Time

Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

Queue Time ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our Zoom app.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App operates entirely within your Zoom client and:

  • Does NOT store any meeting data
  • Does NOT record participant information
  • Does NOT transmit data to external servers
  • Does NOT use cookies or tracking
  • Does NOT require user accounts or registration

How the App Works

Queue Time runs locally in your Zoom meeting client and:

  • Accesses participant list only during active meetings
  • Stores settings temporarily in your browser's local storage
  • All data is cleared when you close the app

Data Access

The App requests these Zoom permissions solely for functionality:

  • View meeting participants: To display names in the queue
  • View meeting information: To verify active meeting status

This information is only used in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App is hosted on Netlify for reliable delivery. Netlify may collect standard web logs (IP addresses, browser types) but has no access to your Zoom meeting data.

Data Security

Since we don't collect data, there is no data to secure. All queue and timer information exists only in your active session memory.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated revision date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: mgallagh@gmail.com

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the App at any time through your Zoom client.
